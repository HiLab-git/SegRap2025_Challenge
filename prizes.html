Prizes

SegRap2025 offers the following rewards:

  • Cash awards and Certificates
  • Co-author with the challenge paper, which will be submitted to Top Journal
Cash awards for each task ($4,000 in total)
  • 1st place for each task: $1,000
  • 2nd place for each task: $600
  • 3rd place for each task: $400
Publication

The top teams will be invited to give oral presentations at the MICCAI DALI workshop as well as the MICCAI2025 Satellite Event. Besides, the top-performing teams will be invited to co-author the challenge paper, which will be submitted to a Top journal (MedIA/TMI).
